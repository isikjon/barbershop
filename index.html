A [barbershop] with character and heritage: a style shaped by travel, mastery, and tradition.

A Swiss space where craftsmanship becomes identity, culture, and experience.

Glaucio’s Barbershop is a place where every cut is shaped by years of practice, travel, and learning from renowned professionals around the world. Its style was forged across Italy, the Netherlands, Brazil, and England, blending traditional barbering with a modern vision of men’s grooming.

Here, individuality and attention to detail come first. The atmosphere is calm and unrushed, designed to understand each client and craft a look that reflects his character. Located in Küsnacht, this barbershop is not only about technique — it’s about trust, aesthetics, and the culture of men’s care.
